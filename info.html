Online Banking Features

  • Bill Pay: Pay bills online to anyone in the U.S. Setup is quick and easy, with delivery options from 1-3 business days.
  • Account Aggregation: View all your financial accounts in one place, even if they're with other institutions.
  • Mobile Check Deposit: Deposit checks instantly using your smartphone's camera.
  • eStatements: Go paperless with electronic statements delivered securely to your inbox.
  • Account Alerts: Receive real-time notifications about account activity, low balances, and more.
  • Transaction Search: Easily search and filter transactions by date, amount, or description.
  • Financial Tools: Access budgeting tools, spending analysis, and savings calculators.
  • Credit Score Tracking: Monitor your credit score and get tips to improve it.

Security & Safety

🔒 Protecting Your Account

Bank of America uses industry-leading security measures to protect your information:

  • 128-bit Encryption: Your data is encrypted during transmission
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Extra layer of security beyond your password
  • Fraud Monitoring: 24/7 real-time fraud detection on your accounts
  • Secure Login: Identity verification before you access your accounts
  • Liability Protection: Federal protection against unauthorized transactions
Security Best Practices:
  • Never share your User ID or password with anyone
  • Use a strong, unique password with mixed characters
  • Log out when finished, especially on shared computers
  • Keep your contact information current
  • Review your account regularly for suspicious activity
  • Enable multi-factor authentication
  • Be cautious of phishing emails and unsolicited calls

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot User ID or Password?" on the login screen, answer security questions, and follow the prompts to reset your password.

Q: Is Online Banking free?

Yes, Online Banking is free for all Bank of America customers. Standard data rates may apply when using mobile devices.

Q: How long do bill payments take?

Typically 1-3 business days depending on the payee. Express payments (1 business day) may have additional fees.

Q: Can I set up recurring payments?

Yes. Bill Pay allows you to schedule automatic payments for regular bills like utilities, mortgage, or rent.

Q: Is mobile banking secure?

Yes. Mobile banking uses the same 128-bit encryption and fraud protection as Online Banking for maximum security.

Q: What if I don't recognize a transaction?

Contact us immediately at 1-800-742-5850. We have fraud protection and can help dispute unauthorized transactions.

Q: Can I access my accounts from outside the U.S.?

Yes, but we recommend notifying us of international travel. Your account may have security restrictions.

Q: How often should I change my password?

We recommend changing your password at least every 90 days for optimal security.
