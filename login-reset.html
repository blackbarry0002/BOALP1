Reset Your Password or User ID

Regain Access to Your Account

If you've forgotten your User ID or password, follow these simple steps to reset your login credentials securely.

Quick Reset Options

Choose the option that matches your situation above, or continue reading for detailed instructions.

What You'll Need

Social Security Number Last 4 digits of your SSN
Account Information Account number or phone number on file
Verification Method Email or phone verification
Valid ID Driver's license or passport info

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Go to the Login Page
    Visit secure.bankofamerica.com and look for the "Forgot User ID or Password?" link.
  2. Choose Your Reset Option
    Select whether you need to reset your password or find your User ID.
  3. Verify Your Identity
    Provide your Social Security Number (last 4 digits) and other personal information to verify your identity.
  4. Select Verification Method
    Choose to verify via email, text message, or security questions you previously set up.
  5. Create New Credentials
    Follow the on-screen instructions to create a new User ID or password that meets our security requirements.
  6. Confirm Your Changes
    Receive confirmation that your reset was successful. You can then log in with your new credentials.

Password Security Requirements

Your new password must meet these requirements:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • Include at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • Include at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • Include at least one number (0-9)
  • Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
  • Cannot contain your User ID or Social Security Number

Security Tips

🔒 Protect Your Account
  • Never share your User ID or password with anyone, including Bank of America employees
  • Use a strong password that's unique to your Bank of America account
  • Change your password regularly (at least every 90 days)
  • Update your contact information to ensure you can verify your identity
  • Enable multi-factor authentication for added security
  • Be cautious of phishing emails asking you to reset your password

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does the reset process take?

A: Most resets are completed instantly. If you choose email verification, you should receive a link within a few minutes.

Q: Can I reset my password from my mobile app?

A: Yes, you can access password reset from the login screen of our mobile app. Simply tap "Forgot User ID or Password?" at the bottom of the login screen.

Q: What if I can't verify my identity online?

A: If you cannot complete the online verification process, you can visit your local Bank of America branch with a valid government-issued ID, or call our customer service team.

Q: Is my account safe if someone resets my password?

A: Our verification process requires personal information that only you should have. If someone gains access to your account, contact us immediately at 1-800-742-5850.

Q: How often can I change my password?

A: You can change your password as frequently as you like. We recommend changing it at least every 90 days for maximum security.
