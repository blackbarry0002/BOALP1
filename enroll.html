What You'll Need to Get Started

Before You Begin

  • Active Bank of America Account - Checking, savings, or credit card account in good standing
  • Social Security Number - For identity verification
  • Valid Government ID - For additional security verification if needed
  • Account Access - Your account number and routing number (found on your checks or statements)
  • Current Contact Information - Phone number and email address

Enrollment Process

1

Create Your User ID

Choose a memorable User ID that you'll use to log in. It must be 5-32 characters and cannot contain special characters.

2

Set Your Password

Create a strong password with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters (minimum 8 characters). This is critical for account security.

3

Verify Your Identity

Complete identity verification using your Social Security Number and personal information. You may be asked to verify a deposit or answer security questions.

4

Choose Security Questions

Select and answer security questions that only you would know the answer to. These help protect your account if you forget your password.

5

Link Your Accounts

Select which Bank of America accounts you want to access online. You can add or remove accounts anytime after enrollment.

6

Start Banking Online

Confirmation will be sent to your email. You're all set! Log in and start enjoying the benefits of online banking.

Enrollment Requirements

Age Requirement: You must be at least 18 years old to enroll in online banking. A parent or guardian may enroll for customers under 18 if they are joint account holders.
Account Status: Your account must be in good standing with no restrictions or holds placed on the account.
Verification: Bank of America may contact you via phone or email to verify your identity as part of the enrollment process.

After You Enroll

✓ What's Available Immediately

View account balances and transaction history • Check pending transactions • Download statements • Look up routing numbers • View account details

✓ Additional Features After 24-48 Hours

Bill pay setup • External account transfers • Account to account transfers • Mobile check deposit • Appointment scheduling

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is online banking safe?

A: Yes. Bank of America uses advanced security measures including 128-bit encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring to protect your accounts.

Q: How long does enrollment take?

A: Enrollment typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete. Your full online banking access may be enabled within 24-48 hours.

Q: Can I enroll if I have multiple accounts?

A: Yes. You can enroll with one User ID and add multiple Bank of America accounts during the enrollment process or anytime afterward.

Q: Do I need to be a specific account type to enroll?

A: No. You can enroll if you have a checking account, savings account, credit card, or other Bank of America products.

Q: Can I use the same User ID for business and personal accounts?

A: No. Business accounts require a separate enrollment with different login credentials for security purposes.

Q: What if I can't enroll online?

A: Visit your local branch or call us at 1-800-742-5850. A representative can help you complete your enrollment.
